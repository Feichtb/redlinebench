### Step 3: Report your findings

Overall, this document set provides a clear design intent but suffers from severe technical and coordination errors that will impede construction. The most critical issues are the structural impossibility of the wall and roof assemblies (sandwiching insulation between studs and sheathing), the omission of code-required egress windows in all bedrooms, and the fundamental conflict between the drawings and specifications regarding the window manufacturer. Significant revisions are required to ensure the building is both safe and structurally sound before permits or bidding can be finalized.
