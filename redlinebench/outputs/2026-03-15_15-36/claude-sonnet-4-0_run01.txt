### Summary

The document set shows comprehensive architectural development but suffers from coordination gaps between drawings and specifications, incomplete detail development, and unresolved design decisions. The three greatest areas of concern are: (1) **specification-to-drawing coordination** with generic drawing callouts not matching specific products described in specifications, (2) **missing construction details** particularly for specialty systems like solar installation, pool construction, and steel-to-wood connections, and (3) **incomplete design resolution** with numerous "TBD" items and missing technical information needed for construction. While the overall design intent is clear, these coordination and completeness issues would likely generate significant RFIs and change orders during construction.
