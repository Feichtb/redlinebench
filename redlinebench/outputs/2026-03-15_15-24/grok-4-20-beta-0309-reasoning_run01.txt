**Summary:** The documents are a preliminary bid set with significant coordination gaps, incomplete code/life-safety information, and many deferred decisions. Greatest concerns are (1) conflicting and incomplete garage fire separation and life-safety details, (2) material and cladding inconsistencies between specifications and drawings (especially west facade), and (3) pervasive use of "TBD"/"verify in field" that will generate RFIs and change orders during construction. The architectural set lacks sufficient integration with the narrative specifications and omits critical assembly details at key transitions.
