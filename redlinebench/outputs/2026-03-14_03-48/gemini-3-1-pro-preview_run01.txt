### Step 3: Summary

The construction document set demonstrates a clear and robust architectural design intent but suffers from significant coordination failures between the drawing annotations and the written specifications. The top three areas of greatest concern are: 1) direct contradictions between the drawings and specs regarding major envelope components, specifically window/door manufacturers and insulation thicknesses; 2) constructability constraints, particularly the lack of spatial clearance for the massive 18-foot garage door header and the physically unsupported lowered shower subfloor detail; and 3) life safety compliance regarding the insufficient ceiling fire-rating assembly specified for the garage located directly beneath a habitable bedroom. Addressing these issues prior to construction will prevent significant procurement delays, RFI generation, and code-compliance failures in the field.
