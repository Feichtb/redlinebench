Summary: The set has a strong architectural design intent but is not fully coordinated for construction pricing or execution. The most serious issues are the envelope/waterproofing gaps, especially low-slope metal roofing, shower waterproofing, and exterior wall/window flashing continuity. The next greatest concern is life-safety/code coordination, especially garage separation, emergency escape openings, safety glazing, and pool/deck guards. The third major concern is drawing/specification conflict: windows, doors, insulation, roofing slopes, stone paving, flooring, and electrical/low-voltage scope all contain discrepancies likely to generate RFIs and change orders.
